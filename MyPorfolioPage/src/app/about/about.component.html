Introduction

I was born and attended primary school in a rural town called Makó, then graduated from high school in Szeged. I moved to Budapest to continue further education at Budapest University of Economic Sciences and Public Administration, where I earned a diploma as Master of International Relations and Economics in the special field of Foreign Affairs and Defence Economics. Then I joined the law enforcement sector and worked for the government as an officer till autumn 2022.

After serving my homeland for almost two decades, my interest turned in new directions. I realized that I read the fresh news of science and technology every day, and I wanted to do something that helps people move onward, or they simply find it useful. I always had and currently still have an attitude of creativity, curiosity, and eagerness to solve complex problems. I searched for the intersection of these sets and came up with the idea of learning IT.

I have started on a small scale: I enrolled in a basic course at Green Fox Academy that was organized for women. I found it interesting, so I sought the possibility of learning more. I gave up my job and started to learn web development at CodeCool.

During my studies, I got to know some programming languages (JavaScript, TypeScript, C#, PHP) and both frontend (React, Angular) and backend (ASP.NET Core, Express.js, Laravel) frameworks. I have earned experience with HTML, CSS, Git, Docker, CI/CD, and deployment.

At this moment, I am on a job hunt. I seek an opportunity to work for a company that values diversity and needs employees with various backgrounds. I can offer hard work, problem-solving skills, openness to continuous learning and new technologies, and the ability to work as a team member.
